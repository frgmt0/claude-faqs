# Code of Conduct

This is a small community project that exists to give people accurate answers when they're confused or frustrated. The conduct bar follows from that purpose.

## Expectations

- **Be welcoming to first-timers.** Most contributors here are making their first open-source PR. Review their work the way you'd want yours reviewed: specific, kind, and about the content.
- **Argue with sources, not volume.** Disagreements about answers are settled by official Anthropic documentation, not by who is most certain. "The support article says X" ends a debate; "everyone knows X" doesn't.
- **No account-shaming.** People show up here because they got banned, billed unexpectedly, or rate-limited. Whatever you think of their situation, the FAQ's job is to give them the next correct step.
- **Respect the privacy basics.** Never post anyone's API keys, emails, invoices, or account details — including your own. Maintainers will scrub and may close issues that do.
- **Stay on mission.** This repo documents reality about Claude and Anthropic's products. It is not a venue for vendor wars, AI doom/boom debates, or campaigning at Anthropic. Take that to the appropriate community spaces.

## Unacceptable

Harassment, personal attacks, slurs, sustained bad-faith argument, spam, and submitting deliberately false information to the FAQ or the submissions API. That last one matters more here than in most projects: people act on these answers.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, close, or lock issues/PRs and revoke API keys at their discretion. Serious or repeated violations get a report to GitHub and a ban from the project. To report a problem privately, contact a maintainer on Discord rather than opening a public issue.

## Scope

Applies to this repository, the FAQ API submission queue, and project-related interactions in community spaces.
